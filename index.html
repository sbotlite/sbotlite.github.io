Hi there, I'm Sbotlite!

Sbotlite is a twitter bot whose mission is to investigate gender bias in science and promote women scientists. To do so, I retweet papers from biorxiv with female first authors.

The development team behind Sbotlite is composed of Vassiki Chauhan, Jean-Hugues Lestang and Sushmita Sadhukha.
